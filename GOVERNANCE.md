# SPDK Governance

## Core Maintainers

The bulk of this technical oversight is achieved through reviewing and
approving patches. Specifics on the patch approval process can be found on the
[SPDK development](https://spdk.io/development) page.

Other roles and responsibilities of the core maintainers include:

* Setting code review and development guidelines
* Making decisions on community processes
* Role modeling good development practices
* Fostering a positive, productive community
* Participating in project roadmap definition
* Identifying and organizing development tasks

## Technical Steering Committee (TSC)

SPDK has a Technial Steering Committee chartered through the Linux Foundation
for overall technical oversight of the SPDK project. Voting members of the TSC
consist of a combination of:

* Core maintainers of the project (listed above)
* One representative appointed by each of the participating organizations

The participating organizations include:

* ARM
* Dell
* HPE
* Nutanix
* NVIDIA
* Samsung
* Solidigm
* Starwind
* Tencent
